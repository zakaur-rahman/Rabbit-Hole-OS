# 🧠 Cognode AI Assistant: Your Visual Thinking Partner

Welcome to the **Cognode AI Assistant**, a powerful agent designed to help you organize chaos into clarity. Unlike traditional chatbots, Cognode AI isn't just here to talk—it's here to **build**. It understands the structure of your thoughts and can manipulate your knowledge graph in real-time.

---

## 🚀 Getting Started

### 1. Opening the Assistant
To open the AI Assistant, click the **Sparkle Icon** (✨) in the top-right corner of your workspace. The Agent Sidebar will slide out, ready for your commands.

### 2. Interaction & Context
The AI is aware of what you are working on. To give it more context:
- **Selected Nodes**: Select one or more nodes on the canvas. The AI will prioritize information from these nodes when responding.
- **Active Board**: The AI always knows which whiteboard you are currently viewing.

---

## 🛠 Core Capabilities

### 🧠 Creating & Brainstorming
You can ask the AI to generate new ideas directly onto your canvas.
- **"Start a node called 'Project Odyssey'."**
- **"Give me 5 sub-topics for 'Sustainable Energy'."**

### 🔗 Linking & Relationship Mapping
The AI excels at finding connections between distant ideas.
- **"Connect the 'Marketing Strategy' node to the 'Budget' node."**
- **"Map the relationship between these three selected nodes."**

### 🔭 Expanding Ideas
Feeling stuck? Use the expansion feature to dive deeper into a concept.
- **"Expand on this idea."** → *The AI will create 3-7 sub-nodes and link them automatically.*

### 📝 Summarizing & Synthesizing
Turn a cluster of notes into a cohesive summary.
- **"Summarize these selections into a single note."** → *The AI will create a summary node and link it to all original sources.*

### 🔍 Natural Language Search
Find what you need without digging through menus.
- **"Find all nodes related to 'Quantum Computing'."**

## 🛡 Safety & Control

We believe you should always be in the driver's seat. The Assistant includes several layers of safety:

1. **Action Preview**: Before the AI makes changes, you'll see a preview of what it's about to do (e.g., "AI wants to create 3 nodes and 2 links").
2. **Confirmation**: Destructive actions, like deleting a node, **must** be confirmed by you before they occur.
3. **Undo System**: Made a mistake? Click **Undo** in the chat panel to reverse the last set of actions instantly.

---

## 💡 Best Practices

- **Be Specific**: Instead of "Make a node," try "Make a note-typed node about the benefits of HIIT."
- **Use Selections**: If you want the AI to analyze specific data, select the relevant nodes first.
- **Iterate**: Don't be afraid to ask the AI to "Refine the last response" or "Make the content more professional."

---

## 🚧 Limitations

- **Complex Layouts**: While the AI can position nodes, it may not create perfect visual layouts for very large graphs.
- **Real-world Actions**: The AI can only manipulate your Cognode workspace. It cannot send emails or browse the live web (yet!).
- **Validation**: AI can hallucinate facts. Always verify important information generated by the assistant.
